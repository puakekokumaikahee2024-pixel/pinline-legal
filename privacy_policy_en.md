# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

Hiroshi Adachi ("we", "us", or "our") establishes this Privacy Policy ("Policy") regarding the handling of user information in the travel itinerary application "Pinline" ("the App") that we provide.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information to provide its features.

### 1.1 Location Information

- **Purpose**: To display your current location on the map, calculate distance and travel time to the next planned activity, and provide automatic check-in functionality upon arrival
- **When Collected**: When using the "Now Here" mode or when tapping the current location button on the map screen
- **Where Sent**: Location information is processed only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties
- **How to Opt Out**: You can disable location services for the App in the iOS Settings app

### 1.2 Photo Library Access

- **Purpose**: To allow you to select photos as cover images for your trips
- **When Collected**: Only when using the cover image selection feature
- **Where Sent**: Selected photos are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to our servers or any third parties

### 1.3 Camera Access

- **Purpose**: To provide photo capture functionality during trips
- **When Collected**: Only when using the camera feature
- **Where Sent**: Captured photos are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to our servers or any third parties

### 1.4 Advertising Identifier (IDFA)

- **Purpose**: To personalize advertisements displayed in the free version of the App and measure their effectiveness
- **Source**: Acquired through standard iOS functionality
- **Where Sent**: Google AdMob (see "Section 3: Third-Party Services" below)
- **How to Opt Out**: You can opt out by disabling "Apple Advertising" or "Allow Apps to Request to Track" in the iOS Settings app

### 1.5 Purchase History

- **Purpose**: To verify and restore premium (one-time purchase) version purchases
- **Source**: Apple's App Store
- **Where Sent**: Managed only on Apple's servers; we only confirm whether a purchase has been made

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect the following information.

- Personally identifying information such as name, email address, phone number, or physical address
- Account registration information (the App does not have an account feature)
- Contact (address book) information
- Trip information, notes, reservation details, etc. that you enter in the App (these are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to our servers)

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services.

### 3.1 Apple MapKit

- Used for map display, location search, and route calculation
- Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

### 3.2 Google AdMob (Free Version Only)

- Used for advertisement delivery and effectiveness measurement
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 3.3 Apple App Store

- Used for app distribution and purchase processing
- Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## 4. Data Storage Location

All data created in the App, including trip information, pins, packing lists, budget information, reservation information, and notes, is stored only on the user's device. It is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

If future versions add data synchronization features using iCloud or similar services, we will update this Policy and notify users accordingly.

## 5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not provide user information to third parties, except in the following cases.

- When the user has given consent
- When disclosure is required by law
- When necessary to protect the life, body, or property of a person

## 6. Data Deletion

Users can delete all data stored by the App on their device by uninstalling the App from their device. The App's settings screen also provides a function to delete all stored data.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. If children under 13 use the App, please ensure they do so with parental consent.

## 8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may revise this Policy as necessary. The revised Policy takes effect when posted in the App and on our website. We will notify users of significant changes within the App.
